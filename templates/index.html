Tips for Safe Medication Use :

  • Store medications in a cool, dry place away from sunlight, and out of reach of children and pets.
  • Follow the dosage instructions provided by your healthcare provider or pharmacist. Do not take more than the recommended dose.
  • Take medication with water or as directed by your healthcare provider. Do not crush or break tablets or capsules unless directed to do so by your healthcare provider.
  • Do not share medications with others. Medications that are safe for one person may be harmful to another.
  • If you experience any unusual symptoms or side effects after taking a medication, contact your healthcare provider immediately.
  • Dispose of medications properly. Do not flush them down the toilet or throw them in the trash. Check with your local pharmacy or healthcare provider for proper disposal methods.
